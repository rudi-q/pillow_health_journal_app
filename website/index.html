Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pillow free to use?
Yes, Pillow is completely free to download and use with all core features included. We're committed to making health management accessible to everyone.
How does Pillow handle my health data?
Your privacy is our priority. All health data is stored locally on your device and is never shared without your explicit permission. We use industry-standard encryption to protect any data that's backed up.
Can I use Pillow for my family members?
Currently, Pillow is designed for individual use. We're working on a family management feature that will be available in a future update.
Does Pillow work offline?
Yes, Pillow works fully offline. Your reminders and tracking features will continue to function without an internet connection.
